## Verification methodology (for next session OR VA continuation)

Each Chrome verification follows this exact procedure. Hand this to a VA — they can run ~25–40 verifications per hour once trained.

### Per-profile checklist (90 seconds each)

1. Navigate to `https://instagram.com/[handle]/`. Confirm page loads (not 404).
2. **Capture**: handle (verified live), follower count (visible), posts count (proxy for activity over time), following count (high following = often less curated audience).
3. **Bio screen**: Does the bio mention pregnancy / baby / newborn / birth / postpartum / motherhood / IVF / NICU / adoption / surrogacy / twin / faith motherhood / loss / rainbow baby as a PRIMARY identity? If only incidental ("mom of two" with primary fitness/fashion/finance content), drop.
4. **Grid scan**: scroll to the first 9 posts. At least 3/9 should be sentimental-keepsake-relevant content (milestones, baby photos, journey, family memory, pregnancy update). If grid is 90% fitness reels or fashion hauls, drop.
5. **Recency**: confirm at least one post in the last 30 days (look for timestamps "1d", "1w", "2w", or a recent month). Dormant accounts drop.
6. **Monetization signal**: highlights like "Shop", "Courses", "Promo Codes", "Sponsors", "Affiliates", "Ambassadors" mean they already do affiliate work — easier yes.
7. **Contact**: bio email > linked website's contact page > IG DM only if A-tier (≤30K). If contact is hidden behind a paywall (Cameo, ChatBoost), drop.
8. **Tier**: A (5K–30K, instant DM target) / B (30K–100K, needs pitch) / C (100K–500K, flat fee + affiliate likely) / C++ (>500K, agency-managed).
9. **Add to verified list** with all captured data.

### Discovery sources (where to find the next 480)

These are the high-yield queues. Walk through them in order:

**1. "Followed by" panels of verified anchor accounts** — When you visit a profile, IG shows "Followed by [3-5 accounts] + N more." The accounts shown are mutual connections in this niche. Click "Followed by ... + N more" → IG shows the full list of YOUR followed-by-them accounts. For each anchor, this surfaces 5-50 peer creators worth verifying.
   - Anchors: @mommy.labornurse, @thelabormama, @thebabychick, @badassmotherbirther, @midwifemarley, @karrie_locher, @lactationlink, @ebbirth

**2. The "Following" list of each anchor** — They follow 700-2,500 accounts each, curated to peers. Scroll the Following list of @midwifemarley (1,969 following) or @anabrandt (7,502 following) for niche peers.

**3. The "Suggested for you" panel** — When you Follow an account, IG shows 5 suggested similar profiles below. Each is a peer creator. Repeat for every verified account.

**4. Hashtag harvest** — IG hashtag pages: #bumpdate, #pregnancyjournal, #firsttimemom, #babymilestones, #pregnancyafterloss, #nicumama, #ivfmama, #twinmom, #adoptionmom, #rainbowbabymama. Sort by Recent. Click into posts → click creator handle → verify.

**5. Brand "tagged-by" pages** — Visit `instagram.com/[brand]/tagged/`. Top-relevance brands: @fridamom, @kindredbravely, @sollybabywrap, @lovevery, @tubbytodd, @lalo, @nunausa, @bobbie, @bumpsuit, @hellobello, @itzyritzyy, @comotomo. Each surfaces 30-100 creators who actively work with parenting brands.

**6. Comment-mining on viral posts** — On a high-engagement post by @thebabychick or @badassmotherbirther, scroll the top commenters. The verified-blue and 5K+ ones are peers.

**7. Podcast guest archives** — The Birth Hour guest list, PedsDocTalk guests, Big Fat Positive guests, Down to Birth guests — each show has had 100+ guests, mostly working creators.

**8. Cofertility's Donor Egg Influencers, Texas Adoption Center's adoption-influencer roundup, Coffee + Crumbs team page** — already mined the top entries; deeper rolls available.

### Pace expectations

- **Per profile**: 90 seconds verified properly.
- **Per hour of focused work**: 25–40 verifications (allowing for screenshot-read-record overhead).
- **To reach 500 verified**: 12–20 hours of focused work.
- **Reasonable split**: 3 sessions of 4–6 hours each, with running file update at end of each session.
- **Or VA assignment**: $7/hr × 25 verifications/hr = $0.28 per verified target. Full 500 = ~$140 in VA cost.

### What to drop

To save time, drop these on the first signal:

- Accounts under 5K followers (even if niche-perfect, audience too small for meaningful conversion)
- Accounts with last post >30 days ago
- Generic mom-lifestyle / mom-fashion / mom-fitness / mom-finance / mom-humor (no sentimental keepsake conversion)
- Coupon-stacking or deal-blog adjacent content
- Anti-vax / conspiracy / MLM-adjacent
- Anything where you can't find a contact method in 60 seconds
