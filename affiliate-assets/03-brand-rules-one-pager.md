# Legacy Odyssey — Affiliate Brand Rules (One-Pager)

Promoting Legacy Odyssey? Please follow these so we stay consistent and compliant.

## ✅ DO
- Describe it as a **digital baby book at your child's own .com domain** (a real website).
- Mention it's **password-protected / private** — parents control who sees it.
- Note you can build it from the **app OR a web browser** — the app is NOT required.
- Use **$29 for the first year** for pricing.
- Always disclose the partnership: **#ad**, **#affiliate**, or "paid partnership."
- Show what a book looks like with the demo: **https://your-family-photo-album.com**

## ❌ DON'T — forbidden words & framing
- ❌ Don't call it a **"family book," "family album," "family story,"** or **"scrapbook."** It's a **baby book about the child.**
- ❌ Don't use the word **"forever"** to describe the **product** (you MAY say the *commission* is "recurring forever" — that's about your earnings, not the product).
- ❌ Don't call sections **"chapters."**
- ❌ Don't use **real children's names** in demos or examples — use a placeholder or your own child. For a live demo, always link **your-family-photo-album.com**, never a real customer's site.
- ❌ Don't run **paid search ads on Legacy Odyssey brand terms**, use **coupon/deal sites**, **incentivize signups**, or **spam**.
