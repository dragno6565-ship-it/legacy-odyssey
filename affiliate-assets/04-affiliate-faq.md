# Legacy Odyssey — Affiliate FAQ (10 Q&A)

Answers you can adapt when your audience asks. Keep the brand rules in mind.

**1. What is Legacy Odyssey?**
A digital baby book built as a real website at your child's own .com domain. Parents add milestones, photos, videos, and letters; family views the finished book at your-childs-name.com. Password-protected. $29 for the first year.

**2. Who can sign up as a customer?**
Any parent, grandparent, or gift-giver. It's built for expecting and new parents, but works for any child.

**3. Do I need the app to use it?**
No. You can build the book from the iOS or Android app **or** from any web browser on a computer — they sync to the same site. The app is convenient for snapping photos on the go; it is not required.

**4. Who owns the .com domain?**
The customer. Their child's name is registered as a real domain at checkout, in their account.

**5. How many photos can you add?**
Plenty — each section holds multiple captioned photos, plus dedicated photo galleries you can create and name yourself (up to 21 photos each).

**6. Can you add videos?**
Yes — "Video Moments" let parents upload short clips (first steps, first words) right into the book.

**7. Can it be given as a gift?**
Yes. There's a gift option — you buy it, the recipient redeems it and sets up their child's site.

**8. Is it private? Who can see the book?**
It's password-protected and hidden from search engines. Only people the parent shares the password with can view it.

**9. How much does it cost?**
$29 for the first year. (There are also annual and longer-term options inside checkout.)

**10. Can customers cancel?**
Yes, anytime from their account. Their content stays theirs.

**11. Do customers ever get refunded?**
No. Legacy Odyssey doesn't offer refunds. If a customer wants to stop being charged, they cancel and keep access for the rest of their paid period. This is part of why your commissions are protected — you don't get clawbacks for refund requests.
