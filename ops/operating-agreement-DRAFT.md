# OPERATING AGREEMENT OF DOR INDUSTRIES, LLC
### A Single-Member Arizona Limited Liability Company

This Operating Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into and effective as of
**[EFFECTIVE DATE]** by **[MEMBER FULL LEGAL NAME]** (the "Member"), the sole
member of **DOR Industries, LLC**, an Arizona limited liability company (the
"Company").

## 1. Formation
The Company was formed by filing Articles of Organization with the Arizona
Corporation Commission under the Arizona Limited Liability Company Act (A.R.S.
Title 29). This Agreement governs the Company's operations and the Member's
rights and obligations.

## 2. Name and Trade Names
The Company's legal name is **DOR Industries, LLC**. The Company may conduct
business under one or more registered trade names ("doing business as"),
including **"Legacy Odyssey"**, as the Member designates.

## 3. Principal Office
The Company's principal place of business is **[PRINCIPAL OFFICE ADDRESS]**, or
such other location as the Member may designate.

## 4. Registered Agent
The Company's statutory agent in Arizona is **[REGISTERED AGENT NAME &
ARIZONA ADDRESS]**.

## 5. Purpose
The Company is organized for any lawful business purpose, including operating
the Legacy Odyssey subscription software platform and related services.

## 6. Term
The Company commenced on the date its Articles of Organization were filed and
shall continue perpetually unless dissolved under Section 14.

## 7. Member
The sole Member is **[MEMBER FULL LEGAL NAME]**, who holds a **100%** membership
interest in the Company.

## 8. Capital Contributions
The Member has contributed initial capital to the Company. The Member is not
obligated to make additional contributions but may do so at any time. A record
of contributions shall be maintained with the Company's books.

## 9. Allocations and Distributions
All profits and losses are allocated to the Member. Distributions are made to the
Member at such times and amounts as the Member determines, subject to applicable
law and the Company's obligations.

## 10. Management
The Company is **member-managed**. The Member has full and exclusive authority to
manage and control the Company, to bind the Company in contracts, and to make all
decisions. The Member may open and operate Company bank accounts and appoint
agents.

## 11. Limitation of Liability and Indemnification
The Member shall not be personally liable for the debts, obligations, or
liabilities of the Company solely by reason of being the Member, to the fullest
extent provided by Arizona law. The Company shall indemnify the Member to the
fullest extent permitted by the Arizona Limited Liability Company Act.

## 12. Separateness (Liability Protection)
The Company shall maintain its own bank accounts and financial records separate
from the Member's personal accounts, shall not commingle Company and personal
funds, and shall observe formalities sufficient to preserve the Company's status
as an entity distinct from the Member.

## 13. Books, Records, and Tax Treatment
The Company shall maintain complete books and records. As a single-member LLC,
the Company is treated as a **disregarded entity** for U.S. federal income tax
purposes (income/expenses reported on the Member's personal return), unless the
Member elects otherwise (e.g., S-corporation election) by filing the appropriate
IRS forms.

## 14. Dissolution
The Company dissolves upon the written decision of the Member or as required by
law. Upon dissolution, assets are applied first to creditors, then to the Member.

## 15. Amendments
This Agreement may be amended only in a writing signed by the Member.

## 16. Governing Law
This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Arizona.
