# Everything Legacy Odyssey Can Do

**Compiled 2026-07-01, verified against the shipped code. For review before it goes on both landing pages.**

## Your child's private website
- Your baby gets their own real website at their own .com web address (e.g. your-childs-name.com)
- Private and password-protected — only the people you choose can see it
- Family and friends visit it in any web browser, on any device — nothing to install
- The custom domain is included and set up for you (name search, registration, and DNS all handled automatically)
- Choose exactly which sections appear; a section shows once you add content to it, and you can show or hide any section yourself
- Site language chooser: greet visitors in English, Spanish, or Hindi — and visitors can switch language themselves
- Measurement units toggle: show your baby's stats in Imperial (lbs / in) or Metric (kg / cm)

## Build it your way — same on every device
- iOS app, Android app, or the web editor — all three do the same thing and stay in sync
- The app is not required: you can build and fill the entire website from the web editor alone

## What you can add and track (grows with your child, year after year)
- Child info: name, name meaning, birth stats, birth city/state/hospital, and the hero photo
- Before You Arrived: the pregnancy, baby shower, nursery, and the wait — reorderable cards plus a checklist
- Birth Story: two perspectives from the day, each with its own headline, narrative, and photo
- Your Birth Day: a captioned photo gallery of the day your child arrived
- Coming Home: the ride home, the first night, and the early days
- Your Journey to Us: an intro, milestone list, and a short letter to your child
- Month by Month: per-month photo, growth stats, and notes (empty months hide automatically)
- Our Family: add each family member with their photo and details
- Your Firsts: first smile, first word, first steps and more — with dates and notes
- Celebrations: holidays, birthdays, and traditions, organized by year, unlimited entries
- Letters to You: write letters your child can read as they grow
- Family Recipes: the dishes that mean home — photos, story, ingredients, and directions
- Their Keepsakes: photos of drawings, awards, certificates, handprints — the physical things you can't keep
- The Vault: sealed letters and memories hidden from viewers until your child's 18th birthday
- Report cards, awards, artwork, milestones and celebrations keep going through childhood — not just year one

## Photos and video
- Add photos throughout every section, each with its own caption
- Upload multiple photos at once
- Custom Galleries: create your own named photo collections with captions and drag-to-reorder
- Reposition photos: drag any photo to set its focal point within its frame
- Video Moments: upload short video clips — first steps, first words, the laugh

## Sharing with family
- Your Contacts: keep a private list of the people allowed to see the website
- Circles: organize contacts into groups like "Grandparents" or "Close Family"
- Send an update: email your contacts when there's something new — each gets their own private link, no password needed
- Text updates to contacts are available in the mobile app

## Gifting and extras
- Buy Legacy Odyssey as a gift, with a personal message and optional scheduled delivery
- Recipients redeem a gift code to claim and set up the website
- Multi-site: one login can manage multiple children's websites and switch between them
- Add another child's site anytime
