## Target Creator Profile

**Sweet spot:** Micro / nano (under 50k followers, ideally with strong engagement).
- New moms documenting their first year
- "Honest mom" / real parenting voice
- Baby product reviewers
- Pregnancy / newborn content creators
- Parenting lifestyle bloggers

**Why micro/nano over macro:**
- Higher engagement rates (3–18% vs 0.5–1% for accounts >100k)
- Cheaper ($50–$300/post vs $1,000+)
- More authentic — followers trust personal recs
- Our product is deeply personal — macro influencer feels wrong

**Disqualifiers (lessons from 2026-06-10 deep-check round):**
- Less than 60% US audience
- More than 40% male audience
- More than 50% audience age 13-24 (not parents yet)
- Engagement rate < 1% on Instagram (suggests bot/inactive followers)
- $500+ per feed post (price doesn't match Legacy Odyssey's $29 product)
- UGC-only profiles (we want their audience, not just content)

## What to offer creators

### Option A — Paid Post ($50–$300)
- Instagram Reel / Photo Feed Post / Story
- Show: claiming the .com + filling the book (app or web editor) + the finished site
- Authentic mom-to-mom tone — NOT ad-read
- Required: tag @legacyodesseyapp, include legacyodyssey.com in bio for 48hr
- Required: content approval before posting (Collabstr enforces this)

### Option B — Free Account (Gifting) via /admin comp gift code
- Gift a free 1-year account (effectively free to us — only Spaceship .com cost ~$11)
- No obligation to post — if they love it, pitch a paid partnership or recruit as affiliate
- High volume play: target 20–30 creators for a creator relationship bank
- Often the better long-term play vs cash

### Option C — Affiliate via Rewardful (35% recurring)
- Hand-off to **affiliates session** when the fit is right
- Lower upfront, but recurring = better long-term creator economics
- See `sessions/affiliates.md` for the Rewardful setup details
