# Legacy Odyssey — Daily Marketing Playbook

> Built 2026-06-27. The "what do I do every day" list. Keep it realistic — ~30–45 min/day beats a 3-hour blitz once a month.
> Log every contact in the **Marketing Tracker** (the CSV / Google Sheet) as you go.

---

## ☀️ EVERY working day (~30–45 min)

1. **Outreach — send 3–5 affiliate DMs.** Work the Tier-A list first (19 creators in `affiliate-assets/tier-a-dm-pack`). Founder-personal, 35% recurring + free comp account. **Log each one** in the tracker. Just 3–5/day = the whole list done in a week.
2. **Engage (10 min).** Reply to every comment/DM on FB + IG. Reply to any influencer/affiliate replies same-day — speed wins these.
3. **Check the scoreboard (5 min).** Any influencer order accepted? Any affiliate sign-up? Any new Stripe sale? Note it in the tracker.
4. **Post or confirm.** Make sure today's scheduled social post went live (or post the queued one). Never a dark day.

## 📅 WEEKLY rhythm (one focus per day)

- **Monday — Plan & Review.** Schedule the week's **4+ social posts** (content-organic + Canva). Review the tracker: who needs a follow-up? What's stalled?
- **Tuesday — Influencer outreach.** Contact 3–5 NEW creators (US-audience first). Add them to the tracker as "contacted."
- **Wednesday — Create.** Batch a week of creatives in Canva; write/advance a blog post.
- **Thursday — Affiliate follow-ups + Pinterest.** Nudge anyone who didn't reply; confirm the pin queue isn't running dry.
- **Friday — Measure.** Pull real numbers (GA4 bot-filtered visitors, Stripe sales). Update the tracker. Plan next week's theme.

## 🔁 The core motion (what actually grows this)

**Ship a feature → blog post → social posts → email customers → influencers/affiliates amplify.** Every new thing the product does should run this loop. It's free and compounding.

## 🚦 Rules of the road

- **No price in any ad creative or post** unless you OK it (hard rule).
- **Ask before using any real outside person's name/handle** in a post (hard rule #13).
- It's a **website, not "a book"**; the sharing feature is **"Your Contacts."**
